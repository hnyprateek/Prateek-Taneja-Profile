Case Study: Route-Level Cost Visibility

A closer look at one program — the Direct Operating Cost (DOC) validation initiative at Air India.

Problem

Aviation fuel and airport charges — two of the largest and most volatile direct operating costs — lacked route-level visibility, making it difficult to validate spend against flight-level economics or spot leakage early.

Action

Designed and deployed a DOC validation program with rigid process controls over fuel tracking and airport charges, integrated with the SAP S/4HANA and AMOS data backbone to tie cost data back to individual flights and routes.

Result
Route-level cost visibility

Finance and operations teams gained a defensible, auditable view of direct operating costs by route — the same cost-control discipline that underpinned the 8% annual cost reduction achieved earlier at IndiGo through route-profitability analysis.
